Tom Cruise's Movies Analysis

Tom Cruise's Information

  • Born: July 3, 1962
  • Year Active: 1981-present
  • Awards: Full list
  • Website: Official Webiste

     With a total of 43 released movies that Tom Cruise acted in, 6 of them are from Mission: Impossible Series. Mission: Impossible first released in 1996. Then Mission: Impossible II and Mission: Impossible III released after 6 years of each. From the timeline, it shows that Mission: Impossible then released other ones within shorter time period (6 years, 6 years, 5 years, 4 years, 2 years, and 1 year). From the imdb database, it shows that Mission: Impossible VII and Mission: Impossible VIII are planning to release in 2021 and 2022.

     From the world map, it shows that America still has the most box office comparing to other countries across the world. Besides this, Mission: Impossible series have higher box office comparing to other movies that Tom Cruise acted in. Also, before 1994, the data only contains America's box office.

     From the radar chart, it compares the average rating, average number of rating, number of movies, average length of the movie in minutes, average numbers of stars in a movie, and average box office between Mission: Impossible series and other movies that Tom Cruise acted in. From that, it shows that the average rating, average length of the movie, and average numbers of stars are about the same. However, Mission: Impossible has more average numbers of rating and box office than other Tom Cruise's movies. Mission: Impossible series is one of the most well-known works of Tom Cruise's.
